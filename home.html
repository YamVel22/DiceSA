How to play?

The objective of the game is to roll the highest number on the dice.

  • The game is played between two players.
  • Players are to take turns rolling the dice.
  • On a player's turn, they roll the dice and the number rolled becomes their score for that round.
  • The player with the highest total score at the end of the game wins.
  • If the player's score is the same then it is a tie.
